Making daily tasks more manageable

washing your hands...



One does not normally think anything of it, that is unless you are limited to just using one hand. What once was a simple task becomes nearly impossible. For the 40 million Americans out there who are limited to using just one hand, no matter the reason, the struggle is all too real.We were amazed at the ingenuity shown in finding a work around, but that got us thinking.

Why should the user have to come up with ways to cope?

Why is their currently no product out there that can solve this challenge without causing a mess and being quite frankly, ineffective.

Without finding any products currently available to meet this need, we knew we had to do something to rectify this problem.

It was that day that Enable Products was founded with the premise of making daily tasks, such as washing your hands, more manageable
