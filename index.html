a new way to wash up

meet nancy

Nancy loves teaching, but does not love the germs that come with it.

She especially does not like it when she accidently brings those germs back to her own family.

Having only one hand, she has found it extremely challenging to get the deep clean she is looking for at the end of the school day.

Without any products available out on the market, Nancy has had to be creative.

Unfortunately these solutions have not been as effective as she would like.

Nancy needs to find a better solution; a new way to wash up.

features

washing has never felt this good!

Ergonomically designed to feel good no matter your hand size. Multiple textures are both effective and fun to use. The silicone bumps massages your hand while it cleans. The long bristles leave you with the tingling feeling that only a deep clean can provide.

finally a product you want to keep out!

Water cascades around the brush like a waterfall. The stainless steel finish looks like a natural extension of your sink. The brush colors can be changed to reflect your personality or the seasons. This is a sink attachment that you do not mind leaving out for all to see (and use!).

your hand has never been so clean

Strong enough to withstand the pressure of scrubbing even the toughest dirt away. The silicone bumps lather and clean the surface of your hand while the gentle abrasiveness of the bristles gives you that deep clean in-between the fingers and underneath the fingernails.
