Hand washing is a very common hygiene practice, but is very difficult to do effectively for a person limited to one hand. As we watched and talked with people who only have the ability to use one hand, whatever the reason, we realized that there is currently no great solution out there. People have found ways to cope, but it is often messy and not nearly as effective as they would like. We knew we had to try and do something to make this process more effective.

For the roughly 40MM people in the U.S that have a disability limiting the use of their hands and the many millions more who self-limit due to multitasking, enAbleÂ® products provide simple, cost effective solutions to allow them to maximize the efficiency with which they perform common household tasks.
